Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This privacy policy explains how our mobile application, Weather Insights, handles your information. We believe in complete transparency and simplicity.

No Data Collection or Processing

We do not collect, save, share, or track any of your personal information. Here are the details:

  • No Personal Information: We do not ask for, collect, or store any personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, or device identifiers.
  • Location Information: The app uses your device's location (GPS) solely to look up the local weather forecast for you. This location check happens directly on your device. We do not store your location, nor do we track your movements.
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account or sign in to use this app.

No Data Sharing

Because we do not collect or store any of your data, we have no information to share, sell, or pass on to anyone else. No third parties have access to your information through this app.
